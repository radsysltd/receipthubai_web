See ReceiptHubAI in Action

Explore the powerful features that make receipt management effortless

Step 1: Organize Your Receipts with Projects

Start by creating a project to keep your receipts organized. Perfect for separating personal expenses, different clients, or monthly tracking. Each project maintains completely isolated data, so you can manage multiple businesses or clients without any cross-contamination

Step 2: Connect Your Email Accounts Securely

Add your Gmail and Outlook accounts using industry-standard OAuth2 authentication. The app only requests read-only access, so your emails stay safe. You can connect multiple accounts from both providers - personal Gmail, business Outlook, client accounts, all in one place

Step 3: Link Accounts to Your Project

Assign specific email accounts to your project. This lets you keep client receipts separate from personal expenses, or organize by business unit. When you search, only the accounts assigned to the current project will be included, keeping everything perfectly organized

Step 4: Find Every Receipt Instantly

Simply check the "Receipts Only" checkbox and click search. ReceiptHubAI scans all assigned email accounts simultaneously, finding every receipt whether it's an attachment or embedded in the email body. Results are sorted by date with easy pagination - no more wondering if you found everything

Step 5: AI Extracts All Receipt Data Automatically

Select the emails you want to process and click "Process Selected". ReceiptHubAI's AI automatically extracts receipt data from email bodies (not just attachments), categorizes each transaction, identifies merchant names, dates, totals, and order numbers, then downloads all attachments in bulk.

Step 6: Review Your Processed Receipts

Switch to the Processed tab to see all your extracted receipt data in one organized view. Each receipt shows its category, merchant name, transaction date, total amount, order number, and an AI-generated summary. Everything is searchable and sortable

Step 7: Export Everything to Excel

Select the receipts you need and export them all at once. ReceiptHubAI creates a comprehensive Excel file with all extracted data, categories, totals, and clickable links to attachments. All attachments are downloaded as organized ZIP files. The Excel file is formatted and ready to import directly into your accounting software - QuickBooks, Xero, or any spreadsheet application

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ReceiptHubAI?

ReceiptHubAI is a desktop application that automates the extraction, organization, and management of financial information from email receipts and invoices. It works with both Gmail and Outlook accounts, uses AI to extract structured data, and exports everything to Excel.

Is ReceiptHubAI secure?

Yes, ReceiptHubAI uses industry-standard security: OAuth2 authentication, read-only email access, local data storage, and privacy guarantees. No email information is shared with any third party except the OpenAI API for processing with the API key set to not retain data for training.

What email providers does ReceiptHubAI support?

ReceiptHubAI supports both Gmail and Outlook/Office 365 including Live, Hotmail, MSN, and your company domain emails if based on outlook. You can connect multiple accounts from both providers and search them simultaneously.
